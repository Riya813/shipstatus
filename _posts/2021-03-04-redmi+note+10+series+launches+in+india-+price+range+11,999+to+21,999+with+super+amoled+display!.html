Xiaomi has finally launched its Redmi note 10 series in India on March 4th. You can watch the live stream of the launch on Xiaomiâ€™s official youtube channel. Xiaomi introduced 3 phones under the Redmi note 10 series - Redmi note 10, Redmi note 10 Pro and Redmi note 10 Pro Max. These phones are very much affordable for the features provided. phones in this series will cost you in the range 11,999 to 21,999.





REDMI note 10

Redmi note 10 price will start at INR 11,999. It has a 6.43-inch(16.33cm)  super AMOLED display with a standard 60 Hz refresh rate. Other features include 48MP Quad camera, 13MP Selfie camera and Corning Gorilla glass protection. It comes with Qualcomm snapdragon 678 processor and massive 5000mAh battery with 33W fast charging which can deliver upto 2 days of battery usage. This phone comes in stunning new colours like Aqua Green, Forest White and Shadow Black.

 

REDMI note 10 Pro

Price range of the redmi note 10 Pro will start at INR 15,999. This phone has 6.67-inch(16.90cm) FHD+ super AMOLED display with billion colours, which comes true to life with 100% DCI-P3 colour gamut. It features a 64MP Quad camera, 16MP in-display selfie camera, 8MP ultra-wide angle lens and 2MP depth sensor for professional photographers. It comes in 3 new exciting colours like Vintage Bronze, Glacier Blue and Dark Night. It has a huge 5020mAh battery with 33W fast charging with 120Hz refresh rate. Redmi note pro is powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 732G processor. 



REDMI note 10 Pro Max

Redmi note 10 Pro Max is a mid premium-segment phone. It features a magnificent 108 MP Quad camera that combines the 3rd generation HM2 108MP primary sensor, 8MP ultra-wide angle lens with 118 degree field of view. We also get to experience life like colours on 6.67-inch(16.90cm) FHD+ super AMOLED ultra-bright display and 120Hz refresh rate with super smooth experience. It is powered by a Qualcomm snapdragon 732G processor and provides a massive 5020mAh battery. It is a thin looking device at 8.1mm thickness and lightweight of 192g. It is available in stunning new colours like Vintage Bronze, Glacier Blue and Dark Night. The regular Redmi note 10 Pro comes with mostly the same features as in Redmi note 10 Pro Max, only difference is Redmi note 10 Pro has 64MP Quad camera, whereas Redmi note 10 Pro Max has 108MP Quad camera. All three phones run Android 11 and MIUI 12 which can be upgraded to MIUI 12.5.
